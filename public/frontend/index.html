HTML-speech-micro-v0.0
PUBLISHED: NOVEMBER 13 2024
This is a multimodal LLM (using Llama-3b as base). It is designed to use html tags to controllably guide the style of the generated speech. It is pretrained on a 2000 hours of audio book data, and finetuned 3 hours of manually collected data, and would cost around $3000 to replicate.
